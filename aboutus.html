Welcome to Edu-ops!

At Edu-ops, we are dedicated to helping you find your true passion and making the most of your educational investments. Our innovative platform offers two key services: personalised counselling by our smart bot to help you discover your field of interest, and a marketplace where you can resell courses at a lower price if you no longer need them.

Discover Your Passion

Our intelligent bot guides you through a series of tailored questions to understand your interests, strengths, and career aspirations. By analyzing your responses, the bot helps identify the fields that align best with your goals, ensuring you make informed decisions about your education and future career.

Resell Unused Courses

We understand that sometimes circumstances change, and you might lose interest in a course you previously purchased. Our platform allows you to resell these courses at a lower price, giving someone else the opportunity to benefit from them. This not only helps you recoup some of your investment but also promotes a sustainable approach to education.
